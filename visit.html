Bright Future Academy 2021

Bright Future Academy prides itself on being a forward-thinking entity, with the constant drive to push boundaries. Thus, it moves onto Phase II of Bright Future Academy acquiring more land in Pitipana, Homagama to deliver greater opportunities to its potential multitudes of students. Along with the new infrastructure, the Faculty of Science, Faculty of Humanity, and Faculty of Law will be added to offer new academic programs to cater to the current trends in academia and its respective industry. Adding on another 15 acres to Bright Future Academy Phase 1 of 25 acres of sprawling space for its students, the new addition would encompass more amenities and facilities to house local and international students including, extended Car Parking facilities, Hostel facilities for over 1,000 students to ensure easy access and comfort, Multipurpose Conference Hall with over 1,500 seating capacity, a large Playground and scenic Walking paths surrounded by a Natural lake.
